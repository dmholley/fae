Faelandia

Winter


The Frost Fae is a winter fairy who creates the frost to cover plants in the winter so they don't die in the freezing winter temperatures. The frost is a thin layer of ice that traps the heat in and keeps the cold out.



The Icicle Fae creates Icicles to hang off of buildings, trees, and other surfaces. Icicles are spikes of ice that form large protruding blocks of ice.



The Glacier Fae takes care of the glaciers and takes them from place to place. Glaciers are slow moving blocks of ice that formed by an accumulation of snow.



The Snoe Fae deals with anything snow. They make sure all snowflakes are different and they spread the snow when winter comes. Snow is a frozen water crystal.
